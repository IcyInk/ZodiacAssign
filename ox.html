The Ox

Second of the twelve animals of the Chinese zodiac, the Ox is a creature embodying diligence, persistence and honesty. Those born in the year of the ox are said to be persistent, simple, honest, and straightforward. “Oxen” are also contemplative and conservative; traits which can make them appear quite stubborn.

“Oxen” have good compatibility with those of the Rat, Monkey, and Rooster years.
