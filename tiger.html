The Tiger

Third of the twelve animals of the Chinese zodiac, the Tiger is a creature embodying bravery, cruelty, forcefulness, and stateliness. Those born in the year of the tiger are said to be tolerant, loyal, valiant, courageous, trustworthy, intelligent, and virtuous. “Tigers” often like to show off and easily wins others trust.

Tigers” have good compatibility with those of the Dragon, Horse, and Pig years.
