The Rat

First of the twelve animals of the Chinese zodiac, the rat is a creature embodying spirit, wit, alertness, delicacy, flexibility and vitality. Those born in the year of the rat are said to be adaptable, smart, cautious, acute, alert, positive, flexible, outgoing, and cheerful. “Rats” often make successful businesspeople as they have excellent social skills.

“Rats” have good compatibility with those of the Ox, Rabbit, and Dragon years.
